   - After `start()`, periodic check runs every 60 seconds (use `vi.useFakeTimers()` and `vi.advanceTimersByTime(60_000)`)

   - `stop()` clears the periodic interval

   - `stop()` is idempotent (calling twice does not error)

   - `start()` calls `audit.flush()` before `verifyChain()` to ensure all buffered entries are on disk before checking chain integrity (this ensures the verification sees the complete chain state)
